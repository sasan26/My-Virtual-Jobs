How to Play

When you start playing this game, you begin with 100 coins, as a gamer or partner user.

There are 2 different type of users : the Gamer and Partner.
On the home page both users can signup and login into their account as shown.

Gamer user:


The gamer page has 3 different subpages: Game console, Profile and Help.


Profile:

There are 4 different sections in the profile page.

Account
Account : here user can change the email or delete the account.


Payment:


Here user can buy more coins, see the payment and income history.
Users can buy more coins by selecting the available coin packages.


There are 2 way to pay, paypal and credit card. You can follow the instruction on the pictures.
Success paypent statuse.


User must click on the “collect button” to save the purchased coins.
Balance: this is the user wallet.
Your degree: here user can see all of the educational degrees that has been achieved from the school.


Help:

There are 4 different pages here also: All Job: there are whole list of the available jobs and their salary. Degree lists: all the available educational degrees are here. Buildings: all the buildings and their detailed information. How to play: (current page).
Game console: This is the main page for the gamer users. There are 16 business buildings and 1 school.


Users need a degree to find a job and make money. Each building needs different type of degrees for each job.
Users can achieve their degrees by attending to school. There are 5 different degrees at school: diploma, associate, bachelor, master and doctoral.


School:

Users must like each image and pay the amount to get their degrees.
At every business building users can get coins by watching videos.


Users must like each image and pay the amount to get their degrees.
Videos are available only for those users that have the requirement degree.


Videos are available only for those users that have the requirement degree.
At the video page users must click on the “green $ button” to start the earning coins process. And after 1 minute the “orange visa button” will be enabled. Users need to click on the visa button to get their coins.


Partner users:

There are 5 different subpages: help, account, ads, analyze and payment page.
Help: has 2 parts: how to play and the job list. On the all jobs page users can see all jobs and their payout rates.
Account: here users can change their email or delete their account.
Payment: here users can buy more coins using their paypal account or credit cards. Also they can see their payment and payout history.



Analyze: here users can see their status of the video ads.
Ads: here user can see their ads, delete them and also add the new ads. Follow the instruction on images.

Gamer users will be paid by their employers, partner users.
